The Smirnoff Nightlife Exchange Project is back for 2011 and with 50 countries taking part it's bigger and better than ever! Once again we're doing a global swap of unique nightlife experiences from around the world and on 12 November, we're going to bring together the best sights, tastes, sounds and of course, drinks, to exclusive venues worldwide.

In 2010 we swapped with Brazil, which saw the samba dancers, drummers and street vendors of Brazil's renowned street parties brought to life right here in Australia. Who are we swapping with this year? You'll just have to wait and see.

For the Nightlife Exchange Project, we're on a mission to discover the world's most original nightlife ideas to bring to life. To get involved, visit our Facebook page and answer questions in the Nightlife Exchange Project tab. Some of last year's ideas involved dance floor lasers and mini chunky beef pies.

Visit the tab, get involved, and help us define what makes Australian nightlife great.

We're thrilled to introduce DJ Anna Lunoe as the 2011 ambassador for the Australian chapter of the Nightlife Exchange Project! She's excited about helping our community define what makes Australian nightlife great and about contributing a few ideas of her own. Welcome Anna, it's great to have you on board.
